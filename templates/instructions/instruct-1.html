Instructions

In this experiment, you will learn 10 different sequences across 5 rounds. There are two sequences per round. Your goal is to learn the correct answers for these sequences to the best of your ability.

Each round will consist of a number of timesteps. At each timestep, you can guess the correct answers for one of the two sequences for the round.

Each sequence will contain a row of boxes, which you can fill using the arrow keys on your keyboard (↑ ↓ ← →).

After each answer, you may get feedback on some parts of the sequence:

Green = Correct. Red = Incorrect. White = No feedback.

You should use this feedback to learn the correct sequence.
