Instructions

In this experiment your goal is to learn sequences of directions (↑ ↓ ← →). Each sequence will contain a row of boxes, which you can fill using the arrow keys on your keyboard.

There are 10 sequences in total, divided across 5 rounds (2 sequences per round). In each round, you will have multiple attempts to get the sequences right. On each attempt, you will be asked to type one of the two sequences for that round.

Here's an example sequence:



After each attempt, you may get feedback on some parts of the sequence:

Green = Correct. Red = Incorrect. White = No feedback.


You should use this feedback to learn the correct sequence.
